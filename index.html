RM
MUSIC
“LOST!” is a single from RM`s solo album “Right Place, Wrong Person”. The song “LOST!” is an alternative pop anthem that resonates with listeners on a deeply personal level. It`s about those who cannot find answers and are lost because of contradictory feelings. The song expresses that even such a situation might be okay when with friends. The lyrics, penned by RM himself, deliver a powerful message of hope, urging those who feel adrift to lean on the support of friends and companionships.

TXT
MUSIC
“Deja Vu” comes in two versions: the original and the Anemoia remix. The lyrics depict a narrative of running away out of fear, but ultimately finding solace and clarity in the familiarity of a deep connection. The concept of “deja vu” reinforces the idea of a love that feels destined and familiar, as if their bond transcends time and space. The term `Anemoia` means nostalgia for a time or place you`ve never experienced. In “Deja Vu,” TXT is desperate to regain the person and the promise they have lost amidst the chaos of youth.”
